📖 About the Book

The Hobbit is a fantasy novel written by J.R.R. Tolkien. It tells the story of Bilbo Baggins, a comfort-loving hobbit who unexpectedly joins a quest to reclaim a treasure guarded by the dragon Smaug.

✨ Favorite Characters

  • 🧙 Gandalf – the wise wizard.
  • 🧝 Thorin Oakenshield – leader of the dwarves.
  • 🧒 Bilbo Baggins – the unlikely hero.
  • 🐉 Smaug – the fearsome dragon.
  • 💍 Gollum – mysterious and unforgettable.

🗺️ The Adventure

From Bag End to the Lonely Mountain, Bilbo encounters trolls, goblins, giant spiders, elves, and discovers courage he never knew he possessed.
