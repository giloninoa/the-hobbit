📖 About the Story

The Hobbit follows Bilbo Baggins, a comfort-loving hobbit who is swept into an extraordinary quest alongside a band of dwarves and the wizard Gandalf.

Along the way, Bilbo discovers hidden strengths, faces dangerous creatures, and learns that even the smallest person can change the course of an adventure.

✨ Beloved Characters

  • Bilbo Baggins – the unlikely hero.
  • Gandalf – the wise and mysterious wizard.
  • Thorin Oakenshield – heir to a lost kingdom.
  • Gollum – a strange creature with secrets.
  • Smaug – the dragon guarding a vast treasure.
